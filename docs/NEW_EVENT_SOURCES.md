## How to Use This Information

### For Manual Event Discovery
1. **Check Instagram accounts directly** - Follow the new local venues for real-time event posts
2. **Search hashtags** - Use `#somosfiesterosrd`, `#eventosdominicanos`, `#costanorterd` on Instagram
3. **Monitor Facebook pages** - Check Anfiteatro PP and Festival Merengue for major announcements
4. **Weekly patterns** - Look for recurring events (Thursday BBQ at Onno's, Wednesday open mic at La Chabola)

### For Automated Ingest
1. The weekly cron job now searches 28 Instagram patterns (vs 7 before)
2. Monitors 48 Instagram accounts (vs 12 before)
3. Checks 12 Facebook event pages (vs 6 before)
4. Uses 10 Dominican search terms (vs 3 before)

### Quality Indicators
**High-quality local events typically include:**
- Spanish-language descriptions with Dominican slang
- Mentions of típico, merengue, bachata artists
- Venue tags in local neighborhoods (not tourist zones)
- Hashtags like `#dominicano`, `#local`, `#costanorte`
- Pricing in Dominican pesos (RD$)
- References to local figures (Luciano Vásquez, local DJs)
